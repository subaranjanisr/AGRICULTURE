Agriculture

Growing Food, Growing Life

What is Agriculture?

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating crops and raising livestock. It is the backbone of our economy and provides food, raw materials, and employment.

Importance of Agriculture

Food Production

Agriculture provides essential food grains, fruits, and vegetables for our survival.

Employment

It is a major source of livelihood in rural areas.

Raw Materials

Industries like textiles, sugar, and paper depend on agricultural products.

Environmental Balance

Sustainable farming helps protect soil, water, and biodiversity.
